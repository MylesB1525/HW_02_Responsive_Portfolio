About Me

Hi there! My name is Myles Burkemper. I was raised in St. Louis and moved to Kansas City and now am proud to call KC home. I started my carrer in sales, which quickly evolved into a love of marketing. My sales and marketing experience has brought me to the University of Kansas boot camp for full-stack web development.

It is here in the boot camp that made me realize my love for design, and where I discovered my passion for writing UX/UI code. JavaScript, HTML, React, and CSS are my favorite languages to writ in so far, but I'm always expanding my knowledge into new technologies. I'm learning quite a few tools in Adobe like XD and Illistrator as well as starting to work in Figma.

When I'm not studying/writing code, you can find me playing roller hockey, riding my bike, hiking, or just about anything outside. I also love playing music. I'm also an avid homebrewer. Once my daughter gets a bit bigger I can't wait to show her my hobbies as well.
